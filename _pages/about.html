Hi, I'm a junior UI engineer working for a media company in Seoul as a member of its digital department. I first started out my journey to become a journalist, but on my way I encountered the beauty of technology. Digital news contents such as NYT <Snow Fall> caught my eye as it successfully utilizes technology to deliver contents more effectively.

Since entering the industry, I've been concentrating on innovating formats of digital content by utilizing digital technology to deliver news context in a more engaging way. My efforts converged into to creating contents in areas of interactive news and data journalism.

As a designer, UI engineer and digital reporter, I cover various work areas to plan, and publish news. Lately, web frontend dev, data handling and data visualization are my major interests.
